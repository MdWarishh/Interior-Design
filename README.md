# 🏠 Interior Design Studio — Premium Portfolio Website

A modern, animated portfolio website built for an interior design studio.
Designed with a premium aesthetic, smooth scroll animations, and a clean
conversion-focused layout — ready to hand off to any interior design client.

## ✨ What Makes It Special

This isn't a template — it's a complete, client-ready product. Every section
is intentionally designed to build trust and drive inquiries:

- **Smooth animations** on scroll — elements fade and slide in naturally,
  giving the site a high-end, editorial feel
- **Premium color palette** — warm neutrals and rich tones that match the
  luxury interior design market
- **Fully responsive** — looks equally polished on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Contact form** — wired up and ready for real client inquiries
- **4 complete sections** — Home, About, Portfolio, Contact — everything a
  studio needs to convert visitors into clients

---

## 🗂️ Sections Breakdown

**Home** — Strong hero with a compelling headline and CTA that immediately
communicates the studio's identity.

**About** — Builds credibility and trust with the studio's story, philosophy,
and what makes them different.

**Portfolio** — Visual showcase of past work, designed to let the projects
speak for themselves.

**Contact** — Clean inquiry form that makes it easy for potential clients to
reach out directly.
